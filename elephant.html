AFRICAN ELEPHANT
(Loxodonta)		 Typical Life Span:
60-70 Years		 Height:
2.50-4 m		 Weight:
2270-6350 kg		 Tusks:
1.5-2.4 m long
  • African elephants are distributed in Sub-Saharan Africa.
  • Elephants exhibit a wide variety of behaviors, including those associated with grief, learning, mimicry, art, play, a sense of humor, altruism, etc.
  • African elephants are highly intelligent. They have a very large and highly convoluted neocortex, a trait they share with humans, apes and some dolphin species.
Conservation Status:
Threatened (Endangered + Critically Endangered)
