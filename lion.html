ASIATIC LION
(Panthera leo leo)		 Typical Life Span:
16-18 Years		 Height:
107-120 cm		 Length:
282-287 cm		 Weight:
100-190 kg
  • The Asiatic lion is surviving today only in India.
  • Lions are the only cats that live in groups, called prides.
  • The most distinguishing characteristic of the Asiatic lion is the longitudinal fold of skin that runs along its belly. This is absent in African lions.
Conservation Status:
Endangered
